Further widening options for potential customers, Tata Motors has introduced Tigor AMT in two variants. The Tigor XTA 1.2 petrol is priced at Rs. 5.75 lakh ex-Delhi, while the top of the line XZA 1.2 petrol has been priced at Rs. 6.22 lakh ex-Delhi. The AMT transmission will be available o.nly with the petrol engine which puts out 85bhp and 114Nm of torque. The XTA variant is pretty loaded with features like the ConnectNext Infotainment system by Harman with four speakers, steering mounted controls and ABS with EBD. Over XTA, the XZA comes with four speaker.
